Listal

One library. Every service. No juggling apps.

A Windows music player that pulls YouTube, SoundCloud, and Bandcamp into a single library. Build playlists that mix any source. Listen along with friends in real time. Discord Rich Presence shows what you're playing, from wherever you're playing it.

# Feature Detail
1 Unified library One list, one search bar, one playlist grid. Mix a YouTube track, a SoundCloud upload and a Bandcamp release in the same playlist and just hit play.
2 Paste to import Drop any track or playlist URL into the search bar. Listal resolves the metadata, caches artwork, and adds it to your library.
3 Listen along with friends Add a friend by @username, hit the Radio button on their row, and hear what they're playing in sync. Auto-loads their next track.
4 Discord Rich Presence Shows title, artist, duration and time remaining. Includes a "Listen on YouTube" button that deep-links to the source.
5 Windows media keys Play/pause and skip keys on your keyboard work everywhere — no need to focus the window. SMTC popup shows title and artwork.
6 Best-available audio Prefers Opus at the highest bitrate the service exposes, so YouTube tracks don't sound like a 128 kbps AAC.
Sources supported
YouTube

Search + paste

Search the whole catalog or paste a video, playlist or channel URL.

SoundCloud

Paste to import

Tracks and playlists from soundcloud.com resolve straight into your library.

Bandcamp

Paste to import

Album pages, single tracks, and artist discographies.

Spotify

Metadata bridge

Paste a Spotify playlist — Listal resolves each track to a YouTube source.

How it works
01

Paste or search

Drop a URL into the search bar or search across services. Listal resolves the metadata locally.

02

Build a playlist

Add tracks to any playlist. Mix services freely — a playlist can be YouTube + SoundCloud + Bandcamp side by side.

03

Just play

Audio streams direct from the source at the best available bitrate. No downloads, no accounts required (except Spotify metadata).
