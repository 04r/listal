# Feature Detail
1 Unified library Everything in one list, one search bar, one playlist. Throw a YouTube video, a SoundCloud upload, and a Bandcamp release into the same playlist and hit play.
2 Paste to import Drop a track or playlist URL into the search bar. Listal grabs the metadata, caches the artwork, and adds it.
3 Listen along with friends Add a friend by @username, hit the Radio button on their row, and hear what they're playing in sync. Their next track loads automatically.
4 Discord Rich Presence Title, artist, duration, and time left. Includes a "Listen on YouTube" button that jumps straight to the source.
5 Windows media keys Play, pause, and skip work from your keyboard even when Listal isn't focused. Windows shows the popup with title and artwork.
6 Best-available audio Grabs the highest bitrate Opus stream the service exposes, so YouTube tracks don't sound flat.
Sources supported
YouTube

Search and paste

Search the catalog or paste a video, playlist, or channel URL.

SoundCloud

Paste to import

Tracks and playlists from soundcloud.com go straight into your library.

Bandcamp

Paste to import

Album pages, single tracks, and artist discographies.

Spotify

Metadata bridge

Paste a Spotify playlist. Listal finds a YouTube source for each track.

How it works
01

Paste or search

Drop a URL or search across services. Listal handles the metadata.

02

Build a playlist

Add tracks to any playlist. Mix services freely: a playlist can be YouTube, SoundCloud, and Bandcamp side by side.

03

Just play

Audio streams direct from the source at the best bitrate. No downloads, no accounts required (except for Spotify metadata).
